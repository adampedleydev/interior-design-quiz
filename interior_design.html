How it works

Purchase your design

Choose whichever design package best suits to you.

Share your vision

Send over your visual details by uploading photos or videos of your space
Include any images of existing furniture you’d like to keep
Share any inspirational photos of designs you already like!
Have you already got measurements of your space? These will help us in

Collaborate Online

Have a video call or phone call with one of our interior designers to go through your needs in more detail.
Shortly afterwards you will receive mood boards, colour schemes and furniture proposals ready for your approval.

It's all in the detail

Once you are happy with what is included in your design, you will then receive (dependant upon your purchase) a 2D or 3D visual of your design.

Shopping time

We create a shopping list of all the items you have confirmed for your design.
You will then receive direct links to your chosen items.
Since we have access to trading accounts, discounts are often available for you to use if you order with us.
